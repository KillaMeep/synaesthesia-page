HyperSekt

HyperSekt is an electronic music project by Denver-based artist and DJ, Lisa Rolfes. The project delves into darker, dystopian soundscapes, blending elements of dark synthwave, industrial, and ambient music to create immersive auditory experiences.

One of HyperSekt's notable releases is the album "Dreaming Out Loud," a collection of downtempo tracks that invite listeners into a parallel universe filled with darkness, mystery, and beauty.
